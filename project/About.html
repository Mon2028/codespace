    • About STUDY SMART

    STUDY SMART was developed by Simon H, a high school student from New Zealand.

    Being a teenager himself, Simon values the importance of education as well as recreation and mental wellbeing.

    So Simon decided to make a change. He created STUDY SMART to help the next generation of students.
    Hopefully, this website has given you enough courage to embark on your educational journey.
